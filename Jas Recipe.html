Taiwan Braised Pork

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pork 300g
Soy Sauce 100g
Sugar 2 Spoons

Step 1: 新鲜五花肉洗干净，擦干水分，放入冰箱冰冻20分钟便于切割，接着连皮带肉切成0.5cm见方的小丁。

Step 2: 锅中加入酱油，八角，酒，冰糖，五香粉，胡椒粉，油葱酥和适量温水，水不要太多，没过五花肉丁1、2cm即可。

Step 3: 大火翻炒均匀后，待烧沸后，将锅中的五花肉丁及汤汁一起倒入砂锅中慢慢卤，2小时后，一锅肉味飘香的卤肉汁就做好了。

Source: 美食天下

Double-skinned Milk

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 500g
Sugar 3 Spoons
Egg White 100g

Step 1: 牛奶加热等等冷却，表面凝结一层奶皮

Step 2: 把凝有皮的牛奶留皮，剩余的牛奶倒入蛋清里面加白糖搅拌（热过的牛奶必须冷了才能跟蛋清一起搅拌否则蛋清会变成蛋花

Step 3: 最后把过滤的蛋清奶倒入原先的碗里面，使原来的奶皮浮起，盖上保鲜膜，放进已沸腾的水里面蒸10分钟，熄火静止5分钟出锅。

Source: 美食天下

Spaghetti w/ Meet Sauce

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beef 1 lb
Diced Tomatoes 29 oz
Spaghetti 3 lbs

Step 1: Drain off any excess fat, then, throw in the chopped celery, onion, and garlic and cook until the vegetables soften.

Step 2: Dump the rest of the homemade pasta sauce ingredients into the pot and bring the mixture to a boil. Once the spaghetti sauce is boiling, reduce the heat and let it simmer on the stove for 3 hours. I know 3 hours sounds like forever, but that’s how long it takes to make a rich, flavorful meat sauce.

Step 3: Once the homemade spaghetti sauce has had time to simmer, all that’s left to do is boiling the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions and dish it up!

Source: foodiecrush
